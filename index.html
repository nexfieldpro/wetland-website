FAQ

Quick answers

How does pricing work?
By volume — how much space your items fill in the truck, from a single-item minimum up to a full load. You get the exact price before we start, and it includes labor, hauling, and disposal fees.
Do I need to be home?
Not always. For curbside and garage pickups, many customers just leave items accessible and pay by phone. For indoor jobs we'll coordinate a time that works for you.
How fast can you come?
Most jobs are scheduled within the same week, and single-item pickups can often be squeezed in sooner. Storm recovery gets priority when the Treasure Coast takes a hit.
What can't you take?
Hazardous materials — wet paint, chemicals, fuel, oil, and asbestos. If you're not sure about an item, call us and we'll either take it or point you to the right disposal site in St. Lucie County.
Do you work with businesses?
Yes — offices, property managers, realtors, storage facilities, and contractors across the Treasure Coast. Volume pricing, after-hours scheduling, and a certificate of insurance are available on request.
Where does my junk go?
As little as possible goes to the landfill. Usable furniture and appliances get donated when we can, metals and electronics are recycled, and the rest goes to licensed disposal facilities.
