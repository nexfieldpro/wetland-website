How we work

We built this business on a simple idea: getting rid of junk shouldn't be a whole project. Send photos, get a real price, pick a time — and it's gone. No site-visit fees, no vague estimates, no surprise charges when the truck shows up.

We're licensed and insured for both residential and commercial work, and we treat every property — from a beach condo on Hutchinson Island to an office suite in Stuart — like it belongs to a neighbor. Because around here, it usually does.

Where things go

As little as possible ends up in the landfill. Furniture and appliances with life left in them get donated locally, metals and electronics go to recyclers, and the rest goes to licensed disposal facilities. It's more work than dumping everything, and it's worth it.

Have a question, a weird item, or a job you're not sure we'd take? Call us — worst case, we'll point you to someone who can help.
