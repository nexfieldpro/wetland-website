FAQ

Fort Pierce junk removal questions

Do you do estate cleanouts in Fort Pierce?

Yes — estate and whole-home cleanouts are one of our most requested services in Fort Pierce. We work room by room on your schedule, keep anything you flag, and leave the property broom-clean. It's a common step before listing older homes for sale.

Can you remove old appliances from Fort Pierce homes?

Absolutely. Refrigerators, freezers, washers, dryers, water heaters, and window AC units are everyday pickups for us. We do all the lifting — just have the unit unplugged and emptied before we arrive, and we handle the rest.

How fast can you get to Fort Pierce?

We're based in Port St. Lucie, about 15 minutes down US-1, so Fort Pierce pickups usually land within a day or two of your quote — sometimes same-day if the route lines up. Photo quotes come back fast, typically the same day you send them.
